The Project Vision

Our focus for this 3000mm kitchenette is durability meeting design. We have specified custom joinery that utilizes moisture-resistant substrates, ensuring that the heavy-use nature of an office environment doesn't compromise the aesthetic over time.

By using 3D modeling, we have pre-planned all plumbing and electrical service points to ensure a zero-clash installation.

Technical Specs

  • Substrate: 18mm MR-MDF (Moisture Resistant).
  • Hardware: Blum Soft-close hinges & Tandembox drawers.
  • Benchtop: 20mm Reconstituted Stone with waterfall edge.
  • Finish: Anti-fingerprint matte laminate.

Expert Knowledge: Spatial Integrity

In a linear 3m span, standard "off-the-shelf" cabinets often suffer from alignment issues. We utilize a custom-engineered sub-frame. This prevents the stone benchtop from bowing over time and ensures that the integrated fridge unit has exactly 50mm of rear ventilation—extending the appliance life by up to 40%.
