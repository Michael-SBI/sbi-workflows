Expert Knowledge: Where Office Fitouts Fail at Handover

The number-one snag list item on commercial fitouts is acoustic bleed between meeting rooms and open workstations. Most builders run partition framing to the ceiling grid only. SBI runs partitions full-height to slab with cavity sound batts at head height and silicone-seals the top track — delivering an effective Rw45 in the room, not just on the spec sheet. Avoids the #1 tenant complaint and the #1 cause of defect liability call-backs.
