Expert Knowledge: Why Hospitality Joinery Fails Early

Most fitouts fail at the kickboard and cookline junction — grease migrates behind standard kick panels, pests establish, and the carcass rots from below within 3 years. SBI specifies marine-ply substrate with sealed, removable kick panels and a continuous silicone seal at the floor interface. Result: the fitout keeps its Food Authority sign-off and visual finish 5–7 years longer than a standard commercial cabinet build.
