Expert Knowledge: What Holds a Feature Joinery Piece Together

On any joinery run over 2.4m carrying stone, the failure point is micro-sag in the cabinet carcass over 12–18 months. The benchtop develops a visible wave, doors stop sitting flush, and the piece loses the premium feel it was sold on. SBI engineers a concealed steel sub-frame tied to wall and slab, and pre-models all service points (power, data, plumbing) in 3D — the piece installs zero-clash in one visit and stays flat for its full lifecycle.
